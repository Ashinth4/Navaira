  • Our Heritage

Built on the principles of regal splendor, Oberoi Palace Udaipur encapsulates the essence of royal living. Originally constructed as a royal residence, the palace has been meticulously restored to its former glory, showcasing intricate marble carvings, majestic domes, and ornate courtyards that reflect the opulence of Rajasthan's royal past. Perched majestically against the backdrop of Udaipur's azure skies, Oberoi Palace Udaipur boasts a rich heritage that echoes the splendor of Rajasthan's royal past. Originally commissioned as a residence for the Maharana of Mewar, the palace exudes a timeless charm that transports guests to an era of regal extravagance. From the intricate marble carvings that adorn its façade to the sprawling courtyards that evoke a sense of grandeur, every corner of Oberoi Palace Udaipur tells a story of opulence and refinement.
